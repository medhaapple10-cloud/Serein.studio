Privacy Policy.

How Serein collects, uses, and protects your personal information. Your privacy is taken seriously here.

Last updated: 1 May 2026
"Your information is yours. Serein collects only what is necessary to serve you and will never sell, share, or misuse it."

Who we are

Serein is a positioning and messaging consultancy operated by Medha Mathur, trading as Serein. References to "Serein", "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Medha Mathur and the Serein practice.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us directly at the details provided at the bottom of this page.

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. This includes:

  • Application information — your name, email address, business details, and responses to the questions on our application form when you apply to work with Serein.
  • Email address — when you download the Alignment Gap Audit PDF or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Communications — any messages, voice notes, or correspondence you send to us during an engagement or enquiry.
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe. Serein does not store your payment card details. Stripe's privacy policy governs how payment data is handled.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is standard web analytics data used to understand how our site is being used.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review and respond to your application to work with Serein
  • Deliver the services you have engaged us for
  • Send you the free resources you have requested (e.g. the Alignment Gap Audit PDF)
  • Send you the Serein newsletter and positioning insights, if you have opted in
  • Communicate with you about your engagement or enquiry
  • Improve our website and service offerings

We will never use your information for purposes beyond what is described here without your explicit consent.

How we protect your information

Every application, every conversation, and every piece of information you share with Serein is treated with complete confidentiality. This is not a legal formality — it is the foundation of the work.

We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS compliant. Email communications are handled through reputable service providers.

We do not store more personal information than we need, and we do not retain it longer than is necessary to serve you.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. Ever.

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our business — such as email marketing platforms (e.g. Mailchimp), payment processors (Stripe), and website hosting services. These providers are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Serein or others.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies — small files stored on your device — to improve your experience and understand how the site is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function as intended if you do.

We use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to email marketing at any time (every email includes an unsubscribe link)
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

Third-party links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or services after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: hello@serein.co
